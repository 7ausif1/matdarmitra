🏢 Stage 2: Entering the Polling Station

The First Polling Officer checks your ID against the electoral roll. The Second Polling Officer applies indelible ink to your left index finger, asks you to sign the register (Form 17A), and issues a voter slip. The Third Polling Officer collects this slip and enables the EVM for you.

Safeguard: The indelible ink prevents multiple voting. Candidates' polling agents sit right there to verify identities and can challenge bogus voters.
